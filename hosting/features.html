The Loruki Platfrom

The Loruki cloud hosting provides you with more than what you can normally expect from bargain-priced hosting plans. On the Loruki website, you’ll see four categories of “WordPress hosting.” You can choose from their four packages: Basic, Plus, ChoicePlus, and Pro. All shared hosting plan includes:

Loruki dedicated server starter plan comes with a Linux operating system, 500 GB SSD RAID storage, 4 GB of RAM, 4 CPU core, and 4 TB of monthly data transfer.

Loruki come with a wide range of features including pre-installed CMS, plug-in updates, enhanced cPanel, CDN, and automatic site backups, among others.

Loruki states that these WordPress plans are “managed” hosting, adding that “our users have SSH access to the virtual instances. Our support technicians ensure that the servers are online and available to the user at all times.”

Loruki staunch supporters of the Open Source community and they provide advanced developer tools like SSH Access, SFTP, and they use a fully-customized Linux kernel on the backend, improving performance and reliability for all sites they host.

Keep in mind that if you use the Cloudflare CDN, that gives you access to Cloudflare’s network of datacenters across the globe. These help deliver content quickly and improve page-load times, regardless of where your customers are located in relation to Loruki's datacenters.

Loruki offers a 30-day money-back guarantee. It’s vital that you read all terms and conditions. When you first sign up, you’ll get a promotional hosting price. But the promotional price only applies to the first term. Then it changes back to the regular pricing.
